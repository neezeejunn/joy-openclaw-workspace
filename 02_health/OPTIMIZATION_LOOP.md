# Health Optimization Loop

## Step 1: Observe
Collect a small number of high-value metrics consistently.

## Step 2: Interpret
Look for weekly patterns, not emotional overreaction to one bad day.

## Step 3: Adjust
Make one or two concrete changes.

## Step 4: Test
Track whether the change improved energy, sleep, stress, body composition, or training consistency.

## Step 5: Keep or discard
Retain what works. Drop what adds friction without benefit.

## Core Philosophy
Health optimization should feel like a smart feedback loop, not punishment.
