# Health Experiment Framework

## Purpose
Use small lifestyle experiments instead of random self-judgment.

## Experiment Structure
- Question: what are we trying to improve?
- Hypothesis: what change may help?
- Action: what exactly will change?
- Duration: how long will we test it?
- Metrics: what will we track?
- Result: did it help?

## Example Experiment Areas
- Sleep timing consistency
- Morning sunlight exposure
- Cutting caffeine after noon
- Increasing protein at breakfast
- Walking after meals
- Reducing screen time before bed
- 3x weekly strength training consistency

## Rule
Only test 1-2 changes at a time.
