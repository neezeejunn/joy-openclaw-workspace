# Health Tracking Framework

## Core Metrics
- Sleep duration
- Sleep quality / recovery
- Resting heart rate
- Body weight / body composition
- Steps / activity minutes
- Strength or exercise sessions
- Nutrition adherence / meal quality
- Energy level
- Mood / stress
- Menstrual cycle or other personal health markers (if relevant)

## Principles
- Start with a small number of high-value metrics
- Track trends, not perfection
- Combine objective metrics with simple subjective check-ins
- Review weekly before making changes
