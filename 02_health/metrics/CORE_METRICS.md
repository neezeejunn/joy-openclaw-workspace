# Core Health Metrics

## Tier 1: Foundational Metrics
These are the highest-value baseline metrics.

### Sleep
- Sleep duration
- Sleep quality / subjective sleep score
- Sleep schedule consistency

### Recovery / physiology
- Resting heart rate
- Heart rate variability (if available)
- Energy level

### Body composition
- Body weight
- Waist or body-fat estimate (if tracked)

### Movement
- Steps
- Exercise minutes
- Resistance training sessions
- Cardio sessions

### Stress / mood
- Stress level
- Mood
- Focus quality

## Tier 2: Lifestyle Inputs
- Meal quality
- Protein intake awareness
- Alcohol intake
- Caffeine timing
- Screen time before bed
- Menstrual cycle or other personal markers (if relevant)

## Tier 3: Optional Advanced Metrics
- Blood pressure
- Blood glucose
- Recovery score from wearable
- VO2 max estimate
- Lab values

## Selection Rule
Start with Tier 1, then add only what improves decisions.
