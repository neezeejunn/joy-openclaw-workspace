# YouTube Build Order

## Goal
Improve YouTube reading in the smallest useful sequence.

## Step 1
Reliable same-day video identification:
- title
- channel
- publish date

## Step 2
Reliable transcript retrieval:
- transcript text
- success/failure detection

## Step 3
Transcript normalization:
- clean text
- remove junk formatting
- preserve useful structure

## Step 4
Structured analysis layer:
- main claims
- sectors
- stocks
- technical levels
- macro points

## Step 5
Reusable output adapters:
- market research comparison
- creator research
- educational summaries

## Rule
Do not build advanced comparison on top of title-only access.
