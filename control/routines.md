# Routines

## Routine 1: US Market Daily
1. Gather index, sector, and key stock moves
2. Gather major market-moving news
3. Judge market regime: risk-on / neutral / risk-off
4. Rank sectors by relative opportunity
5. Select candidate stocks inside preferred sectors
6. Write daily brief
7. If same-day "视野环球财经" content exists, compare convergence/divergence

## Routine 2: Health Optimization
1. Collect available data
2. Update dashboard/log
3. Detect trend changes or weak points
4. Recommend one or two concrete adjustments
5. Track whether adjustments helped

## Routine 3: Information Filtering
1. Ingest new items
2. Tag by source, urgency, value, and actionability
3. Push only urgent/high-value items immediately
4. Queue low-value items for 16:30 batch
5. Summarize and archive where appropriate

## Routine 4: Influencer/KOL Pipeline
1. Discover new candidates
2. Evaluate fit and quality
3. Add qualified profiles to CRM
4. Prepare outreach angle or notes
5. Update status and follow-up timing
