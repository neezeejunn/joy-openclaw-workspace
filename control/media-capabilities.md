# Media Capabilities Roadmap

## Purpose
Define reusable infrastructure for reading and understanding media content, especially YouTube, in a way that can support multiple future workstreams.

## Why this matters
Several planned modules depend on reliable media understanding:
- market research (YouTube finance channels)
- creator/KOL research
- educational content summarization
- future audio/video workflows

## Priority Capability
### YouTube content reading
Current environment can often retrieve titles, but not reliable transcript-grade content.
This is the highest-priority media capability gap to close.

## Design Principle
Build media reading as shared infrastructure, not a one-off feature for one module.
