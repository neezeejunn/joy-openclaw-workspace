# Schedule

- Run US market recap
- Synthesize macro news + sector/stock moves
- Update near-term opportunity view
- If available, compare with same-day content from "视野环球财经"

### Throughout the Day
- Triage incoming information into urgent / important / optional / low-value
- Surface only high-value items immediately

### 16:30
- Review batched low-priority information
- Produce a concise action list or archive recommendations

### End of Day
- Update notes, watchlists, lead lists, and key decisions

### Health

### Information

### KOL
