# Market Analysis Process

## Decision Sequence
1. Determine market regime
2. Identify leading and lagging sectors
3. Check whether leadership is cyclical, defensive, or speculative
4. Use 3-month context to judge continuation vs reversal
5. Rank sectors for the next week
6. Choose stocks within preferred sectors
7. Compare with external commentary without outsourcing judgment

## Stock Idea Standard
Every stock idea should include:
- Thesis
- Why now
- Catalyst
- Risks
- Invalidation / watchpoints

## External Comparison Standard
The channel "视野环球财经" is used only as a comparison layer.
Its view must not override independent analysis.
