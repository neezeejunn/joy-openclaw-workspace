# Output Standard

Every market report should clearly separate:

## 1. Facts
What happened in the market, sectors, and notable stocks.

## 2. Interpretation
What the facts likely mean and how they fit into the 3-month backdrop.

## 3. Opportunity Ranking
Which sectors look best for the next week and why.

## 4. Stock Selection
Which names inside those sectors deserve attention and why now.

## 5. Comparison Layer
How the independent view compares with 视野环球财经.

## 6. Decision Usefulness
What the user should actually watch or do next.
