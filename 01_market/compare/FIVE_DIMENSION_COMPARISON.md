# Five-Dimension Comparison Framework

## Purpose
Compare the channel analysis against the independent report using the same analytical dimensions.

## Dimensions
### 1. Market environment
- Is the overall regime described similarly?
- Is the macro/risk framing aligned?

### 2. Industry / sector view
- Which sectors does the channel appear to favor or avoid?
- Does that align with the independent sector ranking?

### 3. Market data / event calendar
- Does the channel mention relevant upcoming reports, macro events, or timing windows?
- Is its timing emphasis stronger or weaker than the independent process?

### 4. Stock fundamentals
- Are stock mentions supported by business quality, earnings, capital discipline, competitive position, or valuation logic?
- Does the channel rely on narrative more than fundamentals?

### 5. Stock technicals
- Are trend breaks, support/resistance levels, or risk points discussed clearly?
- Does the technical framing agree with the independent view?

## Output Structure
For each dimension, record:
- agreements
- disagreements
- where the channel is stronger
- where the independent analysis is stronger
