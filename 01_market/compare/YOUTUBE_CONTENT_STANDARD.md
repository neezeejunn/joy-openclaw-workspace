# YouTube Content Reading Standard

## Principle
Do not compare against a YouTube video using title alone when producing a serious comparison report.
A formal comparison should be based on actual video content understanding, such as transcript, detailed description, or sufficiently rich content extraction.

## Minimum Requirement for Formal Comparison
Before producing a formal comparison against rhinofinance / 视野环球财经, obtain at least one of the following from the same-day video:
1. transcript
2. detailed video description
3. sufficiently rich content extraction or notes that capture the main arguments

If only the title is available, the comparison should be marked as shallow / provisional and should not be treated as a full comparison.

## Same-Day Rule
Use only same-day market videos for formal daily comparison reports.
If the video publish date is not confirmed, do not use it as the formal comparison layer.
