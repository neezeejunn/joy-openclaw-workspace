# Date Discipline for Reports

## Purpose
Prevent mixing content from different publication dates or market sessions into a single report without clear labeling.

## Required Labels
Every report should distinguish among:
- Report date
- Market session date being analyzed
- News publication date
- Video publication date
- Whether the information is pre-market, intraday, post-market, or background

## Rules
1. Same-day report conclusions must rely primarily on same-day sources.
2. Previous-day or weekend sources may appear only as background context.
3. If a video publication date is not confirmed, do not use it as the comparison layer.
4. If market data timing is unclear, label it explicitly instead of implying same-session precision.
5. Comparison reports require that the channel content and the market/news inputs belong to the same report day.

## Preferred Structure
- Same-day market facts
- Same-day news drivers
- Same-day video comparison
- Background context (clearly separated)
