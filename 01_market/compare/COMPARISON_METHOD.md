# Comparison Method: Independent Analysis vs 视野环球财经

## Principle
The channel is a comparison source, not a decision source.
Independent analysis comes first.

## Workflow
1. Finish independent market view
2. Read/watch same-day channel content
3. Extract the channel's main claims
4. Compare overlap and divergence
5. Judge which view is better supported by data and logic

## Comparison Dimensions
- Market regime
- Macro interpretation
- Sector preference
- Stock selection
- Time horizon
- Risk framing

## Output Sections
- Agreements
- Disagreements
- What the channel got right
- What the channel may be overstating / missing
- Final judgment
