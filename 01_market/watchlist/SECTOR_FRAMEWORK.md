# Sector Opportunity Framework

## Ranking Logic
Score sectors on:
1. Relative strength
2. Fundamental/news support
3. Macro alignment
4. Crowding / valuation risk
5. Near-term catalyst quality

## Priority Tiers
- Tier 1: highest conviction for the next week
- Tier 2: watch closely, trade selectively
- Tier 3: avoid / underweight / too early

## Typical Sector Questions
- Is leadership broadening or narrowing?
- Is this sector benefiting from rates, growth, AI/capex, defense, energy, consumer, or healthcare dynamics?
- Is the move durable or only headline-driven?
- Are there better stocks elsewhere with cleaner setup?
