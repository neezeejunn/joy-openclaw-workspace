# Weekly Execution Plan

## Goal
Identify where the most attractive US investment opportunities may be over the coming week using 3-month context.

## Weekly Steps
1. Review 3-month market/sector trend structure
2. Check whether leadership is accelerating, broadening, or tiring
3. Identify sectors with the strongest combination of momentum + narrative + catalysts
4. Avoid sectors that are overextended, crowded, or losing support
5. Choose a focused stock shortlist within preferred sectors
6. Compare the final view with 视野环球财经's weekly angle
7. Record what would confirm or invalidate the thesis next week

## Weekly Output Standard
- Executive summary
- Sector ranking
- Stock shortlist
- Catalyst calendar
- Risks and invalidation factors
- External comparison
