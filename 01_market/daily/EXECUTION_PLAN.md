# Daily Execution Plan

## Goal
Produce a concise but high-signal US market brief with an actionable 1-week opportunity view.

## Daily Steps
1. Capture market close snapshot
2. Identify index and sector leaders/laggards
3. Note the top market-moving news
4. Determine whether leadership matches macro narrative
5. Rank sectors for the next week
6. Select 1-3 stock candidates inside top sectors
7. Compare with same-day 视野环球财经 content if available
8. Write final brief

## Minimum Viable Inputs
- Index moves
- Sector moves
- 3-5 key headlines
- Same-day channel link/summary (optional but preferred)

## Minimum Viable Output
- 1 market regime call
- 2-3 preferred sectors
- 1-3 stock candidates
- 1 paragraph comparing external commentary
