## 3. Independent Interpretation
The current market is not in a healthy broad-based risk-on regime. The main force right now is the combination of:
- geopolitical energy risk,
- weaker broad index structure,
- and rising selectivity inside the AI / large-tech trade.

That combination favors a market with stronger sector dispersion. Instead of asking whether the whole market is bullish or bearish, the more useful question is: where is relative strength most likely to persist over the next week?
