## 3. Independent Interpretation
This was a strong relief rally, not a clean all-clear signal.

The market is currently transitioning from acute risk-off stress toward a more neutral / cautiously constructive posture. The broad participation in the rally matters, because it shows investors were willing to rotate back into higher-beta areas once geopolitical stress eased. But I do not interpret one broad rebound day as sufficient evidence that the market has fully repaired its structure.

The key takeaway is that the short-term leadership map changed:
- energy lost near-term leadership as oil repriced lower
- growth and cyclical risk appetite recovered
- high-quality technology and consumer discretionary became more attractive again on a short-term basis
