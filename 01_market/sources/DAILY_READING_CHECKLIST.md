# Daily Reading Checklist

## Read First
- CNBC macro / market article
- CNBC market live update or daily open

## Read Second
- Morningstar market overview or featured markets coverage
- Morningstar sector / valuation framing if relevant

## Read Third
- Barron's market-data home
- Barron's economy / futures / relevant linked market context

## Read Fourth
- 1 or more company / stock / sector deep-dive articles from available sources

## Read Fifth
- Same-day rhinofinance / 视野环球财经 video transcript if available and date-valid
- If transcript is unavailable, record failure and skip formal YouTube comparison

## Extract
- Macro driver
- Sector leadership
- Stock shortlist
- Fundamental support
- Technical watchpoints
- Risks
- Comparison points (only with transcript support)
