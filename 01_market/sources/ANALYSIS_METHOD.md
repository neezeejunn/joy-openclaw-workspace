# Analysis Method

## Principle
The report should move from broad to narrow:
1. market regime
2. sector trend
3. stock selection
4. comparison layer

## Analytical Angles
### 1. Industry trend
- Relative strength / weakness
- Macro alignment
- Narrative support
- Risk of overcrowding

### 2. Intra-industry stock performance
- Leaders vs laggards
- Whether leadership is broad or narrow
- Whether performance is justified by fundamentals or only narrative

### 3. Fundamentals
- Revenue / earnings quality
- Capital discipline
- Cash flow strength
- Competitive position
- Balance sheet quality

### 4. Technicals
- Trend breaks
- Key support / resistance references
- Relative strength vs broad market
- Invalidation levels

## Rule
No stock recommendation should appear without at least a brief fundamental and technical rationale.
