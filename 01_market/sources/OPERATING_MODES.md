# Operating Modes

## Mode 1: Semi-Manual (current)
Inputs come from the user through links, screenshots, copied headlines, or notes.

## Mode 2: Assisted Automation
Some sources become stable and repeatable, but comparison/video inputs may still be manual.

## Mode 3: Full Automation
Market data, news intake, and channel comparison are all regularly ingested and scheduled.

## Current Recommendation
Operate in Mode 1 first, prove report usefulness, then automate the most painful inputs.
